Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a flavorful dish made with marinated, grilled chicken pieces simmered in a rich and creamy tomato-based curry. It's a popular dish in Indian cuisine that combines aromatic spices for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, cumin, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until it is slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and mix well to create a smooth sauce.
  6. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
