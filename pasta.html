Simple Pasta

This simple pasta dish is quick and delicious, perfect for a weeknight meal. It features al dente pasta tossed in a flavorful tomato-based sauce with aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

  • 200 g (7 oz) pasta of your choice (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (for garnish)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, chili flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to develop.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley.
